Refinement

Refinement allows you to increase your Equipment's effectiveness. For Weapons, it increases your Weapon ATK. For Armor and Additional Equipment, it increases your DEF. Refinement is very important for survivability. Each DEF on your equipment reduces melee damage by 1%. Each point of refinement on Armor and Additional Equipment also reduces physical damage by 1%.

You can refine your equipment at any Blacksmith. Not all blacksmith accepts all types of Ores for refining. The higher the refinement value, the more difficult it is to increase. The ores used for refining are listed below:

Super Refinement

Super Refinement takes refinement to a new level. Super Refining allows you to increase your weapon's base attack and your armor's base defense. Previously, you could only increase a weapons's base attack and armor's based defense from production. And while you increase it, you also risk breaking and losing the equipment. Super Refinement guarantees that your equipment will not break.

To use super refine your equipment, you will have to refine your equipment to +9. Then speak to Dyron the Blacksmith in Fort Bailune to Super Refine your equipment. You can use Super Refine Gel to increase your success rate. When you super refine, you will lose the +9 whether it succeeds or fails.

Super refinement allows you to achieve the max cap of 95 DEF. This means you only receive 5% damage from all melee damage. This pretty much makes you immune to all melee damage.

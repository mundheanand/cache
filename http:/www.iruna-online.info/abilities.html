☆Ability

Ability allow you to add extra stats onto equipment. Ability come from Boss Drop or from Login Bonus. An Item has an ability if you see the "☆" ability symbol on the item description. They can be transferred to other equipments or leveled up by combining them.

Ability can be leveled up by combining them with a second equipment of the same type and level. On success, the second equipment breaks and disappears. On fail, the first equipment can degrade ATK or DEF and the ability level can degrade. You can add an Ore to increase success rate.

When combining, transfer the second ability to a cheap equipment because it will get destroyed.

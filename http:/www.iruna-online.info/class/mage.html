Mage


Description
Mages are magic casters. They use magic to heal themselves and to attack monsters. Mages are weak because they need to put a lot of their stat points into INT and DEX. INT makes their magic stronger and DEX lets them cast faster.

Must be an Apprentice Mage
Location: Fort Bailune - Varuna

Servant


Description
Servants are a support class that have the ability to control hate in a party. They have the ability to see a monster's weakness and can lend their powers to a member to increase the person's power.

Requirement
Must be level 140
Must be an Apprentice Servant
Location: Capital City Elban - Riaty

Apprentice Servant
Must be level 120
Must be a Cleric
Must have skill: Holy Light 1
Must have skill: Kreis Heal 2
Location: Capital City Saterica - Cleil

Assassin


Description
Assassins have weak defense but they can deal immense damage to enemies using backstab. They can kill most monsters with one hit.

Requirement
Must be level 140
Must be an Apprentice Assassin
Location: Capital City Elban - King Elbano

Apprentice Assassin
Must be level 120
Must be a Hunter
Must have skill: Arrow Rain 2
Must have skill: Venom Attack 3
Must have skill: Impetus 1
Location: Capital City Saterica - Solf

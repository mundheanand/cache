High Wizard


Description
High Wizards utilize the four elements to deal massive magic attack: Meteor, Blizzard, Thor's Hammer, and Earthquake. They are probably the strongest area attacker in the game. They are almost unstoppable when partied with a Bishop.

Requirement
Must be level 140
Must be an Apprentice High Wizard
Location: Capital City Elban - King Elbano

Apprentice High Wizard
Must be level 120
Must be a Wizard
Must have skill: Fire Lance 3
Must have skill: Ice Spear 3
Must have skill: Thunderbolt 1
Location: Capital City Saterica - Nesri

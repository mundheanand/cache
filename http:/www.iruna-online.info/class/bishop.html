Bishop


Description
Bishops are the strongest healer in the game. They can spam heal spells that heal 30k+ HP. They just need to party with a High Wizard for MP.

Requirement
Must be level 140
Must be an Apprentice Bishop
Location: Capital City Elban - King Elbano

Apprentice Bishop
Must be level 120
Must be a Cleric
Must have skill: Holy Light 1
Must have skill: Kreis Heal 2
Location: Capital City Saterica - Cleil

Hunter


Description
Hunters specialize in having high AGI and/or DEX depending on their builds. They can later become Snipers, Assassins, or Ninjas. Many hunters use Claws or Bows.

Requirement
Must be level 70
Must be an Apprentice Hunter
Location: Capital City Sofya - Nepherta

Apprentice Hunter
Must be level 50
Must be a Warrior
Must have skill: Defense 3
Must have skill: Battle Mastery 3
Location: Diel Village - Kady

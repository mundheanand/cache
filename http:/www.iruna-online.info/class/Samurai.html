Samurai


Description
Samurais are a complex hybrid class. They focus on high attack speed, but their primary weapons also reduce attack speed by 100-200%. Blades like Muramasa are built for Samurais. However, a Samurai has a inner talent prevents attack speed from being lower than 70.

Requirement
Must be level 100
Must complete quest: Ganaji Professions
Location: Minaula City - Suigetsu

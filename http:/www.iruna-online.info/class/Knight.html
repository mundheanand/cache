Knight


Description
Knights are strong tank classes. They eventually have very high defense and hp. Later they can become Gladiators, Paladins, or Beast Knights. Many Knights use swords. Many also equip Myrica Wristlets because it increases their VIT by their Level when using Swords.

Requirement
Must be level 70
Must be an Apprentice Knight
Location: Capital City Sofya - Ralgan

Apprentice Knight
Must be level 50
Must be a Warrior
Must have skill: Defense 3
Must have skill: Battle Mastery 3
Location: Rokoko Plains - Venia

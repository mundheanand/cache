Paladin

Paladin


Description
Paladins have the most defense in the game. Most paladins max out VIT and INT making them strong against both melee and magic attacks. When equipped with shields, they can sometimes negate damage and stun monsters. They are also the only melee class that have heal.

Requirement
Must be level 140
Must be an Apprentice Paladin
Location: Capital City Elban - King Elbano

Apprentice Paladin
Must be level 120
Must be a Knight
Must have skill: Double Attack 3
Must have skill: Relax 2
Must have skill: Hard Hit 3
Location: Capital City Saterica - Leyard

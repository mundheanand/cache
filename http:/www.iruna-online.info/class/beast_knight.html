Beast Knight


Description
Beast Knights are a hybrid tank class that specialize in auto attack. Many use Swords or Claws and have high agility for evasion and attack speed.

Requirement
Must be level 140
Must be an Apprentice Beast Knight
Location: Wibo City - Lora

Apprentice Beast Knight
Must be level 120
Must be a Knight
Must have skill: Double Attack 3
Must have skill: Relax 2
Must have skill: Hard Hit 3
Location: Wibo City - Lora

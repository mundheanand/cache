Cleric


Description
Clerics have strong healing magic. Later, they can become Monks or Bishops. Monks are melee fighters that fight with Claws or Throws. Bishops are pure healers that also have strong light attacking magic.

Requirement
Must be level 70
Must be an Apprentice Cleric
Location: Capital City Sofya - Hannet

Apprentice Cleric
Must be level 50
Must be a Mage
Must have skill: Mana Mastery 3
Must have skill: Mana Recover 3
Location: Rokoko City - Maya the Alchemist

Enchanter


Description
Enchanters are a magic melee class that uses magic swords. They have Stone Skin and rely on Autoskill and Additional Magic Attack to deal massive burst damage. Many enchanters use Magic Additional Attack Crystas like: Ifrit, Leviathan, Pretortoise, Cherose, Gewalt and Enfar.

Requirement
Must be level 140
Must be an Apprentice Enchanter
Location: Capital City Elban - King Elbano

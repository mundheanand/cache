Gladiator


Description
Gladiators are strong attackers and strong tanks at higher level. They can toughen their armor with Stone Skin and heal themselves with Bloodthirst.

Requirement
Must be level 140
Must be an Apprentice Gladiator
Location: Capital City Elban - King Elbano

Apprentice Gladiator
Must be level 120
Must be a Knight
Must have skill: Double Attack 3
Must have skill: Relax 2
Must have skill: Hard Hit 3
Location: Capital City Saterica - Leyard

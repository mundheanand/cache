Monk

Monk


Description
Monks are a healing melee class that specialize in Claws and/or Throws. They need high skill delay which enables them to use their strongest attack, Geo Breaker, which is a magic attack. They require high INT for MATK.

Requirement
Must be level 140
Must be an Apprentice Monk
Location: Capital City Elban - King Elbano

Apprentice Monk
Must be level 120
Must be a Cleric
Must have skill: Holy Light 1
Must have skill: Kreis Heal 2
Location: Capital City Saterica - Cleil

Sniper


Description
Archers are a powerful range attacker that have strong area damage. They specialize in using Bows, so they should have high DEX and STR.

Requirement
Must be level 140
Must be an Apprentice Sniper
Location: Capital City Elban - King Elbano

Apprentice Sniper
Must be level 120
Must be a Hunter
Must have skill: Arrow Rain 2
Must have skill: Venom Attack 3
Must have skill: Impetus 1
Location: Capital City Saterica - Solf
